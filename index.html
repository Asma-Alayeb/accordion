Angles

Module 7.1 Exploration

Estimated Time: 2–3 Hours

Why Are We Here?

This lesson covers the definition and measurement of angles, including angles in standard position, positive and negative angles, degree and radian measure, coterminal angles, reference angles, and the unit circle.

Accessibility Note: Closed captions are available on all lecture videos.

Learning Objectives

  • Define angles in standard position
  • Measure angles in degrees and radians
  • Identify positive and negative angles
  • Find coterminal angles
  • Determine reference angles
  • Use the unit circle to interpret angles geometrically
